Frequently Asked Questions


1. Is two-wheeler insurance mandatory in India?

Yes, at least third-party liability insurance is mandatory for all two-wheelers in India. However, we recommend comprehensive coverage for better protection.

2. How can I reduce my two-wheeler insurance premium?

You can reduce premiums by installing anti-theft devices, opting for a higher deductible, maintaining a good riding record, and availing No Claim Bonus (NCB) discounts.

3. What is IDV in two-wheeler insurance?

IDV (Insured Declared Value) is the maximum amount you can claim for your two-wheeler in case of total loss or theft. It's based on the vehicle's current market value.

4. How do I file a two-wheeler insurance claim?

You can file a claim through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our 24/7 customer support. We'll guide you through the entire process and documentation required.

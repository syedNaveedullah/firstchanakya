Types of Life Insurance

  • Term Life Insurance

    Provides coverage for a specific period, offering high coverage at affordable premiums. Ideal for young families and those with temporary financial obligations.

  • Whole Life Insurance

    Offers lifelong coverage and accumulates cash value over time. Suitable for those seeking lifetime protection and a savings component.

  • Endowment Plans

    Combines life insurance with a savings component that matures at the end of the policy term. Perfect for goal-based savings with insurance protection.

  • Unit-Linked Insurance Plans (ULIPs)

    Offers a combination of insurance and investment. Ideal for those looking to create wealth while ensuring life coverage.
